Welcome to The Wizard Files
This website is created keeping in mind the Wikipedia page : List of Harry Potter characters. The Wikipedia page does not have any visual, but is full of valuable information. So, this website presents to you a list of characters from the Harry Potter series. Each character appears in at least one Harry Potter - related book or story by J. K. Rowling. These books and stories include the seven original Harry Potter novels (1997 - 2007), Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (2001), Quidditch Through the Ages (2001), The Tales of Beedle the Bard (2008), Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (2016), Short Stories from Hogwarts of Power, Politics and Pesky Poltergeists (2016), Short Stories from Hogwarts of Heroism, Hardship and Dangerous Hobbies (2016), Hogwarts: An Incomplete and Unreliable Guide (2016) and the Harry Potter prequel (2008).
